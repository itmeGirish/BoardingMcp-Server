PARTIES

JURISDICTION

FACTS OF THE CASE

4. That the Defendant is the absolute owner and in possession of residential Flat No. 301, Green Heights Apartments, HSR Layout, Bangalore, more fully described in the schedule annexed hereto and hereinafter referred to as the “suit property”.

5. That on 15.06.2024, the Plaintiff and Defendant entered into a written agreement to sell, whereby the Defendant agreed to sell the suit property to the Plaintiff for a total sale consideration of Rs. 85,00,000/- (Rupees Eighty Five Lakhs only).

6. That as per the terms of the said agreement, the Plaintiff paid a sum of Rs. 10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakhs only) as earnest money/advance to the Defendant on the date of execution of the agreement, which the Defendant duly acknowledged.

7. That it was agreed between the parties that the balance sale consideration of Rs. 75,00,000/- (Rupees Seventy Five Lakhs only) would be paid by the Plaintiff to the Defendant at the time of execution and registration of the sale deed, which was to be completed within three months from the date of the agreement or within such extended period as mutually agreed.

8. That the Plaintiff has always been and continues to be ready and willing to perform his part of the contract by paying the balance sale consideration and getting the sale deed executed and registered in his favour.

9. That despite repeated requests and reminders by the Plaintiff, the Defendant has failed and neglected to execute and register the sale deed in favour of the Plaintiff. The Plaintiff has also issued a legal notice dated {{Date of Notice}}, calling upon the Defendant to execute the sale deed, but the Defendant has failed to comply.

10. That the Plaintiff submits that the Defendant is now avoiding his contractual obligations and has refused to execute the sale deed without any just or valid reason.

11. That the Plaintiff is still ready and willing to pay the balance sale consideration and perform his part of the agreement, but the Defendant is unjustly refusing to perform his part.

12. That the Plaintiff submits that there is no legal impediment for the Defendant to execute the sale deed in favour of the Plaintiff.

13. That the Plaintiff submits that the agreement to sell dated 15.06.2024 is valid, subsisting and binding on the parties.

CAUSE OF ACTION

14. That the cause of action for the present suit arose on 15.06.2024 when the agreement to sell was executed and continues to arise from the date the Defendant refused to execute the sale deed, and on subsequent dates when the Plaintiff made requests and issued legal notice, all within the jurisdiction of this Hon’ble Court.

RELIEF
