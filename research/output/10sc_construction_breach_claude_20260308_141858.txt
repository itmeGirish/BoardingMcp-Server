SUIT FOR DAMAGES FOR BREACH OF CONSTRUCTION AGREEMENT

MOST RESPECTFULLY SHOWETH:

PARTIES

1. The Plaintiff is an adult Indian citizen, residing at the address stated above, and is the owner of the residential property situated at Survey No. 45, Whitefield, Bangalore.

2. The Defendant is a contractor by profession, residing at the address stated above, and was engaged by the Plaintiff for construction of the Plaintiff’s residential house at the aforesaid property.

JURISDICTION

3. This Hon’ble Court has jurisdiction to entertain and try this suit as the cause of action arose within the territorial jurisdiction of this Court and the suit property is situated within the jurisdiction of this Court.

FACTS OF THE CASE

4. The Plaintiff and Defendant entered into a written Construction Agreement dated {{Date of Agreement}} for the construction of a residential house at Survey No. 45, Whitefield, Bangalore.

5. As per the terms of the said Agreement, the Defendant agreed to construct the house for a total consideration of Rs.1,20,00,000/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty Lakhs only), with the stipulated date of completion being 31.12.2025.

6. The Plaintiff, from time to time, paid the Defendant a total sum of Rs.80,00,000/- (Rupees Eighty Lakhs only) towards the construction as per the agreed payment schedule.

7. The Defendant, after completing only about 60% of the total construction work, abandoned the site and failed to carry out further construction despite repeated requests and reminders from the Plaintiff.

8. The Plaintiff issued a legal notice dated {{Date of Notice}} to the Defendant calling upon him to resume and complete the work, but the Defendant failed and neglected to comply.

9. Due to the Defendant’s abandonment and breach of contract, the Plaintiff was compelled to engage another contractor to complete the remaining 40% of the work.

10. The estimated cost for completion of the remaining work by another contractor is Rs.70,00,000/- (Rupees Seventy Lakhs only), which is substantially higher than the proportionate cost under the original agreement, resulting in a cost overrun and financial loss to the Plaintiff.

11. The Plaintiff has also suffered damages on account of delay in completion of the house, including loss of use, inconvenience, and escalation in construction costs.

CAUSE OF ACTION

12. The cause of action for the present suit arose on {{Date when Defendant abandoned work}}, when the Defendant abandoned the construction work and failed to perform his obligations under the Agreement, and continues to subsist.

BREACH OF CONTRACT

13. The Defendant has committed breach of contract by failing to complete the construction work within the stipulated time and by abandoning the work after receiving substantial payment.

14. The Plaintiff is entitled to claim damages from the Defendant for the breach of contract under Section 73 of the Indian Contract Act, 1872.

DAMAGES CLAIMED

15. The Plaintiff claims the following amounts as damages from the Defendant:

    a. Excess cost incurred for completion of the remaining work by another contractor: Rs.22,00,000/- (i.e., Rs.70,00,000/- minus the balance Rs.48,00,000/- under the original contract);

    b. Damages for delay, inconvenience, and escalation in costs: Rs.10,00,000/- (Rupees Ten Lakhs only);

    c. Total damages claimed: Rs.32,00,000/- (Rupees Thirty Two Lakhs only).

16. The Plaintiff reserves the right to claim further damages as may be ascertained during the course of trial.

NOTICE

17. The Plaintiff issued a legal notice dated {{Date of Notice}} to the Defendant, calling upon him to pay the aforesaid damages, but the Defendant has failed and neglected to comply.

LIMITATION

18. The suit is filed within the period of limitation prescribed under the Limitation Act, 1963.

CAUSES OF ACTION ARE NOT BARRED

19. The Plaintiff states that no other suit or proceeding in respect of the subject matter of this suit has been filed or is pending before any court or authority.

RELIEF SOUGHT

20. The Plaintiff is entitled to recover the said sum of Rs.32,00,000/- (Rupees Thirty Two Lakhs only) from the Defendant, along with interest at the rate of 12% per annum from the date of suit till realization.

21. The Plaintiff is also entitled to costs of this suit.
