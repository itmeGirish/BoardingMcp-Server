LEGAL BASIS

LEGAL BASIS: The suit is founded on the Law of Torts (Defamation), under which a person who publishes false and defamatory statements concerning another, causing harm to reputation, is liable to compensate the aggrieved party. The relief sought includes compensatory damages for reputational injury and exemplary damages where malice or egregious conduct is proven, together with an order for mandatory injunction to remove the defamatory material from the public domain. No statutory defamation code is cited here; relief is sought under the general principles of tort law relating to defamation and associated equitable relief where appropriate.
