PLAINT FOR RECOVERY OF MONEY

JURISDICTION

1. The Plaintiff submits that this Hon'ble Court has jurisdiction to try and entertain this suit under Section 9 and Section 20 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, as the Defendant resides within the territorial limits of {{CITY}}, {{STATE}}, and the cause of action arose within the jurisdiction of this Hon'ble Court.

PAYMENT AND PURPOSE

1. The Plaintiff states that on {{DATE_OF_PAYMENT}}, the Plaintiff paid a sum of Rs. 20,00,000/- (Rupees Twenty Lakhs Only) to the Defendant as an advance amount.

2. The said amount was paid for the purpose of a business transaction between the Plaintiff and the Defendant, wherein the Defendant was to fulfill certain contractual obligations in exchange for the said advance.

FACTS OF THE CASE

1. The Plaintiff states that subsequent to the payment of the advance amount, the business transaction failed due to the default of the Defendant.

2. The Plaintiff states that the consideration for the payment of Rs. 20,00,000/- has totally failed as the Defendant did not fulfill the obligations associated with the business transaction.

3. The Plaintiff states that despite the failure of the transaction and the total failure of consideration, the Defendant has retained the advance amount and has not refunded the same to the Plaintiff.

LEGAL BASIS

1. The Plaintiff submits that under Section 65 of the Indian Contract Act, 1872, when an agreement is discovered to be void or when a contract becomes void, any person who has received any advantage under such agreement or contract is bound to restore it, or to make compensation for it, to the person from whom he received it.

2. The Plaintiff submits that since the consideration for the advance payment has totally failed due to the Defendant's default, the Defendant is legally bound to restore the advantage (advance amount) received from the Plaintiff.

3. The Plaintiff further submits that under Section 34 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, the Court has the discretion to award interest on the principal sum adjudged from the date of the suit to the date of realization, and also prior to the institution of the suit at such rate as the Court deems reasonable.

CAUSE OF ACTION

1. The Plaintiff states that the cause of action for this suit arose on {{DATE_OF_FAILURE}}, when the business transaction failed due to the Defendant's default and the consideration for the advance payment failed, giving rise to the obligation to refund the amount.

LIMITATION

1. The Plaintiff submits that this suit is filed within the period of limitation prescribed under Article 47 of the Limitation Act, 1963, which provides a period of three years from the date when the consideration fails.
