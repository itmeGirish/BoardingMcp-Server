FACTS
1. The Plaintiff is a reputed businessman with established business interests. 
2. The Defendant published false and defamatory statements on social media alleging that the Plaintiff committed fraud in business dealings.

4. On being informed of the posts, the Plaintiff requested a retraction and removal, but the Defendant refused to retract or remove the posts.
