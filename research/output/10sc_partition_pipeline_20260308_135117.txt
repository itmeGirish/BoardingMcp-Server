JURISDICTION

9. This Court has jurisdiction to try this suit under Section 16 and Section 17 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, as the suit properties are situated within the jurisdiction of this Court at Bangalore. {{JURISDICTION_CLARIFICATION}} Section 16 provides that suits for determination of any right to immovable property shall be instituted in the Court within the local limits of whose jurisdiction the property is situated. Section 17 provides that where the property is situated within the jurisdiction of different Courts, the suit may be instituted in any Court within the local limits of whose jurisdiction any portion of the property is situated.

10. This Court also has jurisdiction under Section 9 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, which states that the Courts shall have jurisdiction to try all suits of a civil nature unless expressly barred. The present suit for partition is a civil suit not barred by any law.
