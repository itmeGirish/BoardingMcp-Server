4. LEGAL BASIS — TOTAL FAILURE OF CONSIDERATION AND RESTITUTION UNDER SECTION 65 OF THE INDIAN CONTRACT ACT, 1872

4.1 It is submitted that Section 65 of the Indian Contract Act, 1872 provides:

"When an agreement is discovered to be void, or when a contract becomes void, any person who has received any advantage under such agreement or contract is bound to restore it, or to make compensation for it to the person from whom he received it."

4.2 In the present case, the agreement between the Plaintiff and the Defendant has become void/has totally failed in its consideration due to the default and inaction of the Defendant. The Defendant, having received the advantage of Rs. 20,00,000/- under the said agreement, is bound under Section 65 of the Indian Contract Act, 1872 to restore the same to the Plaintiff.

4.3 In the alternative, and without prejudice to the above, the Plaintiff submits that the Defendant is also liable to pay compensation under Section 73 of the Indian Contract Act, 1872, which provides:

"When a contract has been broken, the party who suffers by such breach is entitled to receive, as compensation for any loss or damage caused to him thereby, such amount as naturally arose in the usual course of things from such breach."

The Defendant, having committed a clear breach of his contractual obligations by failing to perform the agreed transaction despite receiving the advance, is liable to compensate the Plaintiff for the loss naturally arising therefrom, being at minimum the sum of Rs. 20,00,000/- together with interest.

6. LIMITATION

6.1 The present suit is well within limitation. Under Article 47 of the Schedule to the Limitation Act, 1963, a suit "for money paid upon an existing consideration which afterwards fails" is governed by a limitation period of three (3) years. Time begins to run from the date of the failure of consideration.

6.2 The consideration failed on {{DEFAULT_DATE}} and the present suit is being filed within three years therefrom. The suit is therefore not barred by limitation.
