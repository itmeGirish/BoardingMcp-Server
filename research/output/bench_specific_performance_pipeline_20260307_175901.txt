15. The Plaintiff is entitled to compensation under Section 21 of the Specific Relief Act, 1963, in lieu of or in addition to specific performance for the loss suffered due to the Defendant's refusal.

16. The Plaintiff invokes Section 53A of the Transfer of Property Act, 1882, regarding part performance of the contract.
