SUIT FOR RECOVERY OF POSSESSION OF IMMOVABLE PROPERTY AND MESNE PROFITS

MOST RESPECTFULLY SHOWETH:

PARTIES

1. That the Plaintiff is the absolute owner of the commercial property bearing No. 15, MG Road, Bangalore (hereinafter referred to as the "suit property"). The Plaintiff is competent to institute the present suit.

2. That the Defendant is a resident of the above-mentioned address and was permitted by the Plaintiff to occupy the suit property as a licensee for commercial purposes.

FACTS OF THE CASE

3. That the Plaintiff, being the owner of the suit property, had granted a licence to the Defendant to occupy and use the suit property for commercial purposes on terms and conditions mutually agreed upon.

4. That the said licence was purely permissive in nature and did not create any right, title or interest in favour of the Defendant in respect of the suit property.

5. That the Plaintiff, by notice dated 01.06.2025, revoked the licence granted to the Defendant and called upon the Defendant to vacate and hand over peaceful possession of the suit property to the Plaintiff forthwith.

6. That despite the revocation of the licence and service of notice, the Defendant has failed and neglected to vacate the suit property and continues to occupy the same without any authority of law.

7. That the continued occupation of the suit property by the Defendant after revocation of the licence is illegal and unauthorized, and the Defendant is a trespasser with effect from 01.06.2025.

CAUSE OF ACTION

8. That the cause of action for the present suit arose on 01.06.2025 when the Plaintiff revoked the licence and called upon the Defendant to vacate the suit property, and continues to subsist as the Defendant has not vacated the premises till date.

JURISDICTION

9. That the suit property is situated within the territorial jurisdiction of this Hon’ble Court and the cause of action has arisen within its jurisdiction. This Hon’ble Court has pecuniary and territorial jurisdiction to entertain and try this suit.

RELIEF CLAIMED

10. That the Plaintiff is entitled to recover possession of the suit property from the Defendant, who is in unauthorized occupation thereof.

11. That the Plaintiff is further entitled to claim mesne profits for the period of unauthorized occupation by the Defendant at the rate of Rs. 50,000/- (Rupees Fifty Thousand only) per month from 01.06.2025 till the date of actual delivery of possession.

12. That the Plaintiff undertakes to pay the requisite court fee on the claim for mesne profits as may be determined by this Hon’ble Court.

13. That the Plaintiff has not filed any other suit or proceeding in respect of the subject matter of the present suit.

PRAYER

WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff respectfully prays that this Hon’ble Court may be pleased to:

a) Pass a decree for recovery of possession of the suit property bearing No. 15, MG Road, Bangalore, from the Defendant in favour of the Plaintiff;

b) Pass a decree for mesne profits at the rate of Rs. 50,000/- (Rupees Fifty Thousand only) per month from 01.06.2025 till the date of actual delivery of possession, with liberty to the Plaintiff to apply for ascertainment of the exact amount under Order XX Rule 12 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908;

c) Award costs of the suit; and

d) Grant such other and further reliefs as this Hon’ble Court may deem fit and proper in the facts and circumstances of the case.

INTERIM RELIEF

14. The Plaintiff also prays for such interim orders as this Hon’ble Court may deem fit in the interest of justice.
