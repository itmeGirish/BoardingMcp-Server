LEGAL BASIS

Defamation is a civil wrong governed by the Law of Torts (Defamation). The Plaintiff seeks monetary damages for damages to reputation and exemplary damages for willful harm, along with mandatory injunctions to remove the defamatory material. The relief sought is grounded in common law principles of compensation for harm to reputation and the Courts equitable jurisdiction to restrain and remove defamatory material published online. The claim rests on established tort principles that publication of false statements to third parties constitutes defamation and entitle the plaintiff to damages as compensation and, where warranted, exemplary damages for malice and reckless disregard for the truth.
