JURISDICTION

1. This Court has jurisdiction to try and entertain this suit under Section 9 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, as it is a civil suit of a nature cognizable by Civil Courts.

2. This Court has jurisdiction under Section 16 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, as the suit pertains to immovable property situated within the local limits of this Court's jurisdiction.

PLAINTIFF'S RIGHT AND DEFENDANT'S INTERFERENCE
